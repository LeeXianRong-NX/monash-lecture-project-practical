
    1) Enter name for your superhero character.
    2) Choose a superpower.
    3) Both superhero and villain will have 3 HP.
    4) Select a superpower value to play against the 
       villain every turn - ROCK, PAPER, SCISSORS!
    5) Whoever loses the turn will lose 1 HP.
    6) Whoever wins with their chosen superpower 
       (critical hit), the loser will lose 2 HP.
    7) Nobody will lose HP if both draw with the same 
       superpower value chosen.
    8) Whoever loses all HP first will be the loser, 
       the other will be the winner.

                    [S] Start Game
                    [Q] Quit

            Press matching key to continue
